Game Design Document

- Carrom

Questions

  1. Write the title of your project

    Ans. The title of my Project is Carrom. It is a well-known game based on correct aim and angle.

  2. What is the goal of the game?

    Ans. The goal of the game is to collect most coins before the other player and score as much as possible, to win.

  3. Write a brief story of your game.

    Ans. My sister was not able to play carrom on the real board due to its big size. Due to COVID, we could not buy her a new one that fits right for her. She gets bored everyday. So, for this reason, I wanted to make carrom. She will even learn aiming a coin on the carrom board.

  4. Which are the playing characters of this game?

      Information:

    • Playing characters are the ones who respond to the user based on the input from the user.
    • Cars, monkeys, dinos, wizards, etc., are the playing characters in the game.

    Ans.

    Sr. no. Chracter Name What can this character do?
    1 Striker This character can hit other coins on the carrom board and move them to get them into the pockets.

  5. Which are the Non-Playing Characters of this game?

    Ans.

    Sr. no. Chracter Name What can this character do?
    1 Black Coins This coin can give you points according to the type of the carrom game. According to this type of carrom game, the black coin gives you 10 points when hit into a pocket.
    2 White Coins This coin can give you points according to the type of the carrom game. According to this type of carrom game, the white coin gives you 20 points when hit into a pocket.
    3 Queen This coin can give you points according to the type of the carrom game. According to this type of carrom game, the queen gives you 50 points when hit into a pocket and given a cover coin.
    4 Pockets This character will vanish coins hitting it and increment the score according to the type of coin hitting the pocket

    Altough these characters are the root of the game and make big difference, they are not controlled by the player.

  7. How do you plan to make your game engaging?

    Ans. To make the game more engaging, there are lots of ideas I have, a few of them will be added in the game.
    1. Online 4:4 carrom game
    2. Professional Black and White Game
    3. Tournaments with many games and mutiple players
    4. Multi-player integration in game
      • Four player, or
      • Two player
    5. Positive Feedbacks to player so that he/she feels good.
    6. Rankings all over the world.
    7. High score amongst all players
    8. Realtime Player chat so that there is conversation between the players. Types:
      • Text chat
      • Emoji chat
    9. Friend invitations
    10. Friend message chat
    11. Account integration in game. Reasons:
      • Players can save their playing history
      • They have a safe of their previous matches
      • Their previous chat is intact and friend invitation gets possbile. Reasons:
        • User does not have to invite his/her friend(s) to the game every time they wanna play.
        • Without account, the information of friends and their chat is not visible
      • Gives professionalism to the game
    12. Allowing Guest user. Reasons:
      • Players who don't want to play much and are just for light play don't have to create an account
      • Some people who want to play more than just rare, and not want to share their information with others, can enter guest mode so that their information is not saved.
      • People who don't want to be much social for friend and invitations, can use guest mode.
      • There can be personal reasons for people to have guest mode. But there is no offense to anyone to use guest mode or logged in mode.
